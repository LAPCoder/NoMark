NoMark lang

The NoMark lang is a language for simplifying HTML, SVG, XML, PHP...
It's tranpiled in HTML, SVG, XML, PHP... The extension is .{yourLang}.[nm, nom, nmk, nomk, nomark]. For example, index.html.nom or page.php.nomk. It will be tranpiled respectively in index.html and page.php.
